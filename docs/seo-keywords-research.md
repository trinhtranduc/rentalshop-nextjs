## General SEO Strategy

### Target Search Intent
- Informational: "how to", "guide", "what is"
- Commercial: "best", "review", "compare"
- Transactional: "buy", "pricing", "features"

### Content Clusters
1. Getting Started (Articles 1)
2. Operations Management (Articles 2-5)
3. Business Growth (Articles 6-9)
4. Software Solution (Article 10)

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Article 1 → Articles 2-9 (foundation)
- Articles 2-9 → Article 10 (solution)
- Article 10 → Articles 1-9 (features support topics)
- Cross-link related topics (e.g., inventory → order processing)
